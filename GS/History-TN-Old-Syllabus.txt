* Pre-historic age in Tamil Nadu
=================================
1) The early history of human beings is called the pre-historic age. Written records are not available for the pre-historic period. However, the pre-historic people had left many things such as pieces of pottery, stone and metal tools, simple drawings, bones and skeletons. These materials provide some clue to know the history of the pre-historic period.
